# StayClean — Focus & Win

A Chrome extension that tracks the avoidance of distracting websites and rewards you with points for every 5 minutes you stay away. Built for people who want to break compulsive browsing habits — porn, gambling, alcohol sites, social media, or anything else you keep going back to without meaning to.

---

## What it does

- Add any URL to your block list. When you visit it, a full-screen overlay stops you and asks if you really want to continue.
- Every 5 consecutive minutes on non-blocked sites earns you **10 points** (more with an active streak).
- Points build your **rank** — from Seedling all the way to *3 Years — Free*, which unlocks a special celebration screen.
- A **streak** tracks how many consecutive clean 24-hour days you've had. Break it and your level resets — but your rank and total points are always safe.
- 190 unique motivational quotes, one per day, cycling through the year.

---

## Rank system

400 points = roughly 1 clean day of active browsing. The ranks are designed so most people feel genuine progress in the first few weeks, with the later milestones being a real multi-year commitment.

| Rank | Points | Equivalent |
|---|---|---|
| 🌱 Seedling | 200 | ~half a day |
| 🔰 Starter | 400 | 1 day |
| 💼 Determined | 1,200 | 3 days |
| 🎯 Focused | 2,800 | 1 week |
| ⚡ Disciplined | 5,600 | 2 weeks |
| 🛡️ Warrior | 12,000 | 1 month |
| 🏅 Champion | 36,000 | 3 months |
| 💎 Elite | 72,000 | 6 months |
| 🌅 1 Year Clean | 146,000 | 1 year |
| 🌠 2 Years Clean | 292,000 | 2 years |
| ♾️ 3 Years — Free | 438,000 | 3 years |

## Privacy

No data is collected. No servers. No accounts. No tracking of any kind.

Everything is stored locally in your browser via `chrome.storage.local` and never leaves your device. Logging out permanently deletes everything — your right under GDPR Article 17.

This extension requires no privacy policy because it processes no personal data outside your own device.

---

## Install (Developer Mode)

1. Download or clone this repo
2. Go to `chrome://extensions` in Chrome, Edge, or Brave
3. Turn on **Developer mode** (top right)
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the `stayclean` folder
5. Click the 🛡️ icon in your toolbar to get started
